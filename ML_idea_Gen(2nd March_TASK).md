College – Student Performance Prediction
Input: Attendance percentage, study hours, assignment marks, and internal assessment scores.
Output: Prediction of the student’s final grade or pass/fail result.

College – Placement Prediction
Input: CGPA, number of projects completed, internship experience, and technical skills.
Output: Prediction of whether a student is likely to get placed during campus recruitment.

Healthcare – Disease Risk Prediction
Input: Age, blood pressure level, cholesterol, medical history, and lifestyle habits.
Output: Prediction of the risk of developing a disease.

Healthcare – Patient Readmission Prediction
Input: Patient age, diagnosis, previous hospital visits, treatment history, and recovery condition.
Output: Prediction of whether the patient might need to be readmitted to the hospital.

Shopping – Product Recommendation System
Input: User browsing history, past purchases, product ratings, and search behavior.
Output: Recommended products that the customer may be interested in buying.

Shopping – Customer Purchase Prediction
Input: Customer demographics, browsing activity, time spent viewing products, and past buying patterns.
Output: Prediction of whether the customer will purchase a particular product.
