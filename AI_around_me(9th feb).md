AI Around Me
1. Google Maps – Uses machine learning to analyze traffic data and predict the fastest route.
2. YouTube Recommendations – Machine learning studies watch history to recommend videos.
3. Netflix Suggestions – ML analyzes viewing behavior to suggest movies and shows.
4. Email Spam Filters – ML classifies emails as spam or legitimate.
5. Voice Assistants – ML processes voice commands using speech recognition.
6. Instagram Feed – ML prioritizes posts based on user interaction.
7. Online Shopping Recommendations – ML suggests products based on browsing history.
8. Face Unlock – ML recognizes facial features to unlock smartphones.
9. Language Translation – ML translates text between languages.
10. Smart Typing / Autocorrect – ML predicts the next word while typing.
